CachePut trait - equivalent to Spring’s @CachePut CachePut trait - 等价于Spring的@CachePut

Unlike @Cacheable, @CachePut always executes the method and puts the result in the cache.

与@Cacheable不同，@CachePut总是执行方法并将结果放入缓存。
