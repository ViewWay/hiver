1//! Backpressure (overflow) strategies for hot sources.
2//! 热源的背压（溢出）策略。
3//!
4//! Mirrors Project Reactor's `OverflowStrategy`.
5//! 对标 Project Reactor 的 `OverflowStrategy`。
6
7/// How a hot source (e.g. [`Sinks`](crate::Sinks)) behaves when its internal
8/// buffer is full and a producer tries to emit more.
9///
10/// 当热源（例如 [`Sinks`](crate::Sinks)）内部缓冲区已满、生产者尝试继续发射时
11/// 的行为。
12#[derive(Debug, Clone, Copy, PartialEq, Eq, Default)]
13pub enum BackpressureStrategy
14{
15    /// Buffer everything in an unbounded queue. **Memory risk** if the consumer
16    /// can never keep up. This is the default to preserve all data.
17    /// 在无界队列中缓冲所有内容。若消费者永远跟不上则有**内存风险**。这是默认值，以保留所有数据。
18    #[default]
19    Buffer,
20
21    /// Drop the newest value when full.
22    /// 满时丢弃最新值。
23    Drop,
24
25    /// Drop the oldest value when full (keep the newest).
26    /// 满时丢弃最旧值（保留最新）。
27    DropLatest,
28
29    /// Fail immediately with an [`Overflow`](crate::ReactorError::Overflow) error.
30    /// 立即以 [`Overflow`](crate::ReactorError::Overflow) 错误失败。
31    Error,
32
33    /// Block the producer until space is available (cooperative pull).
34    /// **Not** supported by [`Sinks`](crate::Sinks); use a bounded channel instead.
35    /// 阻塞生产者直到有空间（协作式拉取）。[`Sinks`](crate::Sinks) **不**支持；请改用有界 channel。
36    Block,
37}
